Look, it's just a simple puzzle - the way puzzles should be, you know?

You’re given a set of pieces and an empty outline. No bells and whistles or any of that crazy stuff. It's just you and the pieces, and then that satisfying click when something finally fits right.

Drag each piece into the correct space. Rotate when you need to. Put it together piece by piece, one move at a time.
