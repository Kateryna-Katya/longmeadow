It starts gentle, then gets interesting.

Early puzzles use simple geometry with fewer pieces, so you settle into the rhythm. After that, the shapes begin to curve. Angles get deceptive. Pieces start looking similar on purpose.

  • Beginner

    Low

    4-6 pieces

  • Intermediate

    More pieces. Irregular shapes. Some rotation required. Takes a bit more thought.

    10–20 pieces

    High

    Complex outlines with many small pieces. Requires careful planning and precise placement.

  • Expert

    Very High

    Intricate patterns. Unusual piece shapes. Tight fits. The most challenging puzzles available.
